## `// About`

**Project Dynamica (Pty) Ltd** is a South African deep-tech firm delivering algorithmic trading systems, fintech product architecture, and AI-driven automation tools — with a deliberate focus on emerging market applicability.

We operate at the intersection of quantitative finance and technology infrastructure, building systems rigorous enough for institutional application yet lean enough for agile deployment in African market contexts.

> *"The edge is not in the data. It's in the engine that processes it."*
